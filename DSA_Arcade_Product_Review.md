### What's New & Improved: A Leap Forward in Visualization

Based on invaluable feedback and our relentless pursuit of an intuitive learning environment, we've implemented critical updates that address key areas for our newest customers and project testers:

#### 1. **Graph Traversal (DFS) - Fully Operational!**
*   **Enhancement:** Our Graph Module now features a fully implemented and visually guided **Depth-First Search (DFS)** algorithm.
*   **User Benefit:** You can now explore the intricacies of both BFS and DFS, observing step-by-step how these powerful traversal methods navigate complex graph structures. Visual highlights and clear log messages demystify each stage of the algorithm, ensuring a comprehensive understanding.

#### 2. **Linked Lists - Crystal Clear & Responsive Visualization!**
*   **Enhancement:** The Singly and Doubly Linked List modules have received a complete overhaul in their rendering and user interaction, addressing prior concerns about visibility and intuitiveness.
*   **User Benefit:**
    *   **Dynamic Camera System:** No more "off-screen" elements! The camera now intelligently **auto-adjusts its zoom and position** to ensure that your entire linked list is always perfectly centered and visible, regardless of how many nodes you add.
    *   **Intuitive Pointers (HEAD, TAIL, NULL):** We've replaced abstract 3D text with a more intuitive system. "HEAD" and "TAIL" are now represented by clear **2D HTML labels coupled with distinct 3D arrows** that explicitly point to the first and last nodes. Similarly, NULL pointers are clearly visualized with red boxes and corresponding arrows.
    *   **Enhanced Clarity for Doubly Linked Lists:** Forward and backward pointers now have **increased visual separation**, making it easier to distinguish the direction of traversal and the dual-link nature of doubly linked nodes.
    *   **Seamless Cleanup:** Resetting your list now ensures all visual elements, including labels and arrows, are meticulously cleared, providing a fresh and uncluttered workspace every time.
